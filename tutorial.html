What To Do

Throughout this map there are 3 objects that you have to find.
Once you have found these objects the light pole in the middle of the map will light up indicating you have completed this small game.

Interacting with items

When you find one of the objects that you can interact with a cursor will appear in the middle of the screen indicating that it has some kind of interaction.
To interact press the button on the Google Cardboard or mouse1.

Moving

To move around the map, click and hold mouse1 or the button on the Google Cardboard and a trajectory line will appear showing where you would end up once you release the button.
Rotate your head or click and drag with the mouse to look around.
