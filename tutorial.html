Little Boxes is currently in BETA

As this VR game is currently still in BETA this is not a complete game & expect some bugs.

What To Do

Throughout this map there are little boxes that you have to find.
Once you have found these boxes interact with them to see if you have found the correct box to complete the game.

Interacting with items

When you find one of the objects that you can interact with press on it.
To interact press the button on the Google Cardboard or left click.

Moving

To move around the map, click and hold left mouse button or the button on the Google Cardboard and a trajectory line will appear showing where you would end up once you release the button.
Rotate your head or drag with the mouse to look around.
